Qwinto Game Rules

  • Objective: Fill your rows with numbers from left to right in strictly increasing numerical order.
  • Dice Selection: Choose 1 to 3 dice to roll. You can only enter values into rows whose matching color die was rolled.
  • Placement Constraint: No two rows can contain the exact same number in the same column alignment.
  • Scoring Columns: If an entire vertical column of 3 cells is completed, you score the full value of the cell located inside the pentagon space!
  • Scoring Rows: Filled rows earn points equal to the rightmost number. Unfinished rows score 1 point per filled square.
  • Misstep Penalties: If you roll the dice on your turn and cannot place the sum anywhere, you must click 'Pass' to accept a Misstep (-5 points).
